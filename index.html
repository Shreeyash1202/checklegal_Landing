Understanding the DPDPA

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) is a comprehensive data protection law that aims to safeguard individuals' personal information in the digital age. It sets guidelines for the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data by organizations.

As legal professionals, ensuring compliance with DPDPA is crucial for protecting your clients and their businesses from potential legal issues and reputational damage.

Why is DPDPA Important?

  • Protects individuals' right to privacy
  • Establishes clear rules for data handling
  • Promotes trust between businesses and consumers
  • Aligns with international data protection standards
  • Imposes significant penalties for non-compliance
